AWE GEMS 2014: What is Computer Science?

Today: Stacks!

Leaders: Katie Gibson, Alex Roederer, and Jennifer Paykin

Towers of Hanoi: http://scratch.mit.edu/projects/25009615/

Goal: Move all the rings from the first pole to the third pole.

Rules:

  • You must remove the top ring before any of the lower rings on a pole.
  • You can never put a larger ring on top of a smaller ring.
  • You can only move one ring at a time

Stack Calculator: http://scratch.mit.edu/projects/24614783/

Goal: Understand how to use a simple calculator using stacks.
